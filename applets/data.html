Applets

Some of the coding projects that I’ve made and thought others might enjoy. Have a look at the code if you’re interested in how these work. Everything here was made with Wilson, a library I wrote to make polished, interactive, parallized web applets easier to create.

Stills and animations from these applets have been featured in the 2022, 2023, and 2024 Bridges conferences, the Jordan Schnitzer Museum of Art, the Erb Memorial Union, the 2020 UO Research as Art Competition, the Girls’ Angle Bulletin magazine, and UO’s University Hall.
